# Roadmap

basicbots roadmap is a combination of
- bug fixes
- enhancements
- Better error reporting
- [long term goals](#long-term-goals)


## Long Term Goals
1. `INPUT` & `PRINT` intergration for colaberation between two robots. 
2. More looping routines beyond `FOR`, `NEXT`,`STEP` & `TO`
